Background

My name is Andrew Reid and I'm an economics student at Brigham Young University. I've lived in a number of places including Chicago, Tokyo, Houston, Provo and soon to be Frankfurt, Germany where I will be serving a mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints.

My family is probably the most important thing to me. There are 6 members of my family, all of whom I love very much, even though we haven't taken a family portrait in 14 years. My Dad, Kyle Reid, works in consulting. My Mom, Tami Reid, just finished a masters in healthcare administration. My older sister, Taylor, is currently pursuing a masters in Psychology at Portland State University. My older brother, Eric, is a stats major here at BYU, and my little sister, Addi, is in the BYU Choir.

As far as myself goes, I like to think of myself as a pretty laid back guy. My dream job is to be a writer on SNL, but more practically I want to go work for a consulting firm internationally.
